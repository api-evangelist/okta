Okta's developer team rebranded from "Developer Advocacy" to "Builder Advocacy," reflecting how the builder community now spans traditional developers, AI builders, and no-code/low-code automators. The post cites GitHub Octoverse 2025 data showing 36 million new developers joined GitHub in 2025, with nearly 80% using Copilot in their first week.
